TO ADD PROFESSOR OAK IMAGE:

1. Save the Professor Oak image (the one you showed me - the anime style Professor Oak in white lab coat)
2. Name it: professor-oak.png
3. Place it in this folder: public/professor-oak.png

The image should be a PNG with transparent background for best results.

Recommended sources:
- Bulbapedia: https://bulbagarden.net/
- Search for "Professor Oak Let's Go Pikachu Eevee PNG"
- Or use any Professor Oak anime image you prefer

Once added, the image will appear on the landing page to the right of the Pokédex (on desktop) or below it (on mobile).
